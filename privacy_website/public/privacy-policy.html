1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information that we request will be retained on your device and is not collected by us in any way.

To function as a video teleprompter and editor, the app requires the following permissions:

  • Camera Permission: Required to record video while you read your script. The video data is processed locally on your device.
  • Microphone Permission: Required to record audio along with your video.
  • Storage / Files Permission: Required to save the final video files to your device's gallery or internal storage so you can access, edit, and share them.
  • Internet Permission: Required to enable optional Google Drive cloud backup and to show ads.

We do not upload your videos, scripts, or audio recordings to any external server unless you explicitly enable the Cloud Backup feature. All content created within ScriptCam remains 100% private on your device unless you choose to use Cloud Backup.

2. Google Drive Cloud Backup (Optional Feature)

ScriptCam offers an optional Cloud Backup feature that allows you to back up and restore your scripts, settings, and recorded videos using your personal Google Drive account.

What data is backed up:

  • Your scripts (title and content)
  • App settings (teleprompter preferences, backup preferences)
  • Recorded videos (if you enable "Backup Videos")

How it works:

  • You must explicitly sign in with your Google account to use this feature.
  • All backup files are stored in a private, hidden app-specific folder in your Google Drive (called appDataFolder). This folder is not visible in your regular Google Drive and cannot be accessed by other apps.
  • We do not access any other files in your Google Drive.
  • You can disconnect your Google account at any time from the Settings screen, which stops all future backups.

Google Account Information:

  • When you sign in with Google, we access your name, email address, and profile picture to display your connected account in the Settings screen.
  • This information is stored locally on your device for display purposes only.
  • We do not store or transmit your Google account credentials to any server operated by Primexia Studios.

Data Deletion:

  • You can delete your backup data at any time by disconnecting Google Drive in Settings and then deleting the app data from your Google Drive account settings.

ScriptCam's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
